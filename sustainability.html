Our journey to sustainability

We’re excited to be a part of the sustainability movement, and we’re dedicated to creating a safe future for generations to come.

Safe period products

At Sokoru, we have set out to create period products that are safe for your body and the environment. We are achieving this goal by offering best-in-class products that are free of toxins, free of single-use plastics, and have nothing but the highest-performing materials. We are here to provide sustainable period products so women can menstruate worry-free.

Eco-friendly packaging

We designed our packaging to protect your products and the planet. To be absolutely sure our packaging creates as little waste as possible, we got rid of anything extra.

There are no paper flyers, everything you need to know has been printed on the box. The box is made of recycled cardboard that you can confidently toss in the blue bin to be recycled again. Inside the box, there are no frills, no tissue paper, and no plastic wrapping. When you order online from us, all you’re going to get is your products in a box.

1% for the planet

The leaders of 1% of the Planet think that all businesses profit off resources provided by the planet, so all businesses have to protect the planet. We agree wholeheartedly that our earth requires constant care, so we’ve signed up to donate 1% of gross sales.

We have chosen to help SeaTrees plant kelp forests off the coast of California. Hundreds of species of ocean wildlife appreciate kelp, and sadly these forests are diminishing at a dangerous rate. Our team is grateful to SeaTrees for taking action to restore underwater ecosystems and reverse the effects of climate change before it’s too late.

The road ahead

We are aware that we are not perfect when it comes to sustainability, so we would like to keep you posted on improvements that are not yet here but are coming up next.

To be sustainable means that we must take full responsibility for where our products are ending up. We will be embracing the concept of a circular economy by implementing a return and recycle program soon. The goal is to keep all period products out of the landfill and out of the oceans.

For sustainable period products to become the norm, we need as many women as possible to participate. To make change happen, we plan to expand our size range rather than expand our style range. It’s important that women with all body types can join the movement so stay tuned for plus sizes and petit sizes to come.
